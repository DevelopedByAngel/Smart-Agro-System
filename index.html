Agriculture is main stay of India’s economy. Agricultural development is therefore a precondition of our natural prosperity.Execution of Internet of Things(IOT) in the agricultural sector has brought in a major development . Smart Agro System as the name suggests solves your agricultural problems using the technology of “Internet Of Things(IoT) and Machine learning ".SAS contains Multiple Features. Irrigation System, Mineral detection System and The Pest Management System.

A solar powered Water pump is used to supply water to the fields whose activity will be governed by the farmer from any part of the globe.The values of water content and temperature are tracked lively and sent to farmer’s mobile phone through an app and maintained in a database. Based on the notification sent regarding the humidity of the soil the farmer can switch on/off the motor and control the irrigation pattern of his land.When the water level is critically low the motor turn on automatically.Additionally the minerals present in the soil can are detected and the fertilizers are recommended based on the minerals result.

Various Iot sensors placed in the field helps the farmer to detect and monitor the advent of the pest in the field.The information given by the sensors acknowledge the pest conditions to the farmer. The farmer can monitor the field through the images sent by the system.Once the image of the pest is captured it is compared and verified with various past, pest advent incidents all across the globe by using machine learning technology, which in turn suggests pesticides and other remedies followed in those past events to control the pest. Hence it is a tool in solving a key issue in agriculture.
